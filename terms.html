Welcome to Bloom Finance

We at Bloom want to change the way pay-check advances are done by offering 0% interest on every advance we do. We beleive that pay-check advances should be to help people get on their feet and get their money fast. Not bury them under debt and interest payments. We charge a $15 processing fee that you pay only once.

Qualifications for Advances

Bloom Financing will offer paycheck advances to any employee of any participating employer. We will advance only hours already worked and in your employers system. In order to always qualify for an advance when needed, it is important to report hours regularly to your employer, if a time-punch system is not in place. Furthermore, if the employee has any witholdings such as child support, wage garnishments, alimony, etc. we will deduct that from their maximum loan estimate.

Payback Period

Bloom Financing allows a payback period of one pay cycle. The amount of your advance, plus the $15 processing charge (and delivery charge if you require a hand delivered check) will be automatically deducted from your next paycheck. You do not need to write any checks or make any payments to us. That will happen via your employer automatically. At this time we do not offer a multiple-paycycle payback period.

What are the costs?

We charge a $15 processing charge per advance. We charge a $3 delivery-charge if you need a paper check delivered to you or your office. There are no additional charges for direct deposit. There are no interest payments or late payment charges. There are no hidden fees, charges, or payments of any kind with Bloom Financing. We promise.

When will I get my money?

We process all applications same day and we gaurentee your money in your account the next business day. If you required a delivered check, we will deliver it to your office the next day by end of business day. However, first time advances require HR approvement, so the first time can take a little bit longer.

When do applications need to be submitted by?

This is different for each company. Talk to your HR representative to find out at what time applications need to be handed in for each pay period.

What's my APR rate?

Because we don't charge interest our APR is different for each advance. However, your true cost of an advance is $15. You will not be charged more than that. If you'd like an APR quote please feel free to contact us at any time and we will get that taken care of for you.

How to get started

To get started using Bloom Pay-Check Advances, just visit your HR representative and fill out the brief form. Return it to your HR representative and we will process your advance that same day to make sure your money is waiting for you the very next business day. We can either deposit it directly into your account or cut you a check. Whichever is easiest for you. Go to the bottom of the 'about us' page for step by step instructions on how to get started.

Payment
Payment is made by Bloom Financial automatically deducting that cost from your next paycheck, including any fees or costs of the advance, which includes the $15 processing fee and the $2 surcharge if a physical check is required.
